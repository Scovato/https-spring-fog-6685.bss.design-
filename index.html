Thrifting

...because full price stinks

Why is thrifting better?

Thrifting is better than buying full price...but why? Thrifting allows us to give old things new chances. We can also save money when we thrift rather than buy new. You can save the big bucks on good brands by buying them slightly used. Thrifting may be another man's trash, but it is certainly my treasure!

Help the cause...

Thrifting is a solution to a problem much bigger than ourselves. Big name brands have control over manufacturers and cause us to pay too much for clothes that would be affordable otherwise. Because of this price increase, people are forced to pay too much for clothes that are not good quality. Everybody needs clothes and everybody needs good quality clothes.

Thrifting shows love!

Thrifting is good for the environment and other people. Thrifting helps us apply reduce, reuse, recycle to our everyday lives. This saves the planet and helps us show our love to others!
